How I Think

PM principles I live by

Perspectives shaped by 8 years of shipping real products — not borrowed from a course.

01

Ship to learn,
not to show.

A working prototype validated in 48 hours tends to answer questions that 50 slides can't. The GitHub builds on this page were running before any stakeholder deck existed for the same idea.

Execution over Theater
02

Data tells you what.
Users tell you why.

SQL and Mixpanel reveal patterns — user interviews explain them. Neither is sufficient on its own. A tight cohort analysis paired with a handful of honest conversations usually surfaces more than a 20-page research report.

Mixed Methods Always
03

Strategy is the art
of ruthless no's.

Every yes is a tax on the most important yes. The right roadmap is usually shorter than what stakeholders propose. Good prioritization means being deliberate about what doesn't make it — and being able to explain why clearly.

Prioritization First
